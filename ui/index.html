Halo Orbit Simulation of JWST at L2

Numerical simulation and visualization of JWST's transfer and halo orbit near L2. Explore trajectories, frames, and 3D context in a lightweight gallery.

Team 15

Tianyang Hu · Mark Zhang · Haoran Yan · Shiqi Fan

Transfer (Fixed/Inertial Frame)

JWST's Earth→L₂ trajectory shown in the inertial frame, where both Earth's revolution and the spacecraft's path are visible. This view highlights the absolute geometry of the transfer relative to the Sun-Earth system.

Transfer & Halo Context (Rotating Frame)

JWST's trajectory rendered in the Sun-Earth rotating frame, centered near L₂. With the bulk orbital motion removed, the approach to L₂ and halo dynamics become clearer.
